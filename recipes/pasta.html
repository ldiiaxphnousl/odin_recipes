Fettuccine alfredo

Try this pasta recipe and make the italians proud!

If you ever struggle of think "hmm... what should I make for lunch?" PASTA. Pasta is always the answer.


Ingredients

  • 400 g fettuccine
  • 150 ml double cream
  • 75 g parmesan
  • 20 g butter
  • 1 tsb grinded black peppercons
  • a handful flat-leaf parsley, chopped
